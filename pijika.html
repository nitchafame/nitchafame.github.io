NIDNUENG–PIJIKA

2011–Animation, Stop Motion

 

Nidnueng is the music video that expressing playful and joy visuals represent the meaning of the song is about people who tend to look happier and younger when they are falling in love. The music video uses combination techniques of cel animation and stop-motion to emphasize the feeling of whimsical and delightful. The team making this project are a team of females illustrators and animators that have honestly presented a female point of view.
