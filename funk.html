FUNK

2017 – Creative Direction, Graphic Design, Illustration

 

Album artwork design for Notapol Srichomkwan special EP 'FUNK.' The visual represents the collaboration aspect on the EP that each song has different songwriters, rappers, and singers to add colors and elements to the project. Lead design and art direction, including setting the style for the photoshoot and 360° illustrations for promoting the EP.
