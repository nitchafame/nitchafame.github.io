REVOKE–SINGULAR

2012 – Co-Directed, Animation

 

“Revoke” is an official music video for a track from an alternative metro acoustic band, Singular’s 2012 full album called ‘Spirit.’ The music video is about a relationship between two lovers and how they zen on the awkwardness of their collapsing feelings. The piece used the visual to show blurry nostalgia for past moments they had together, using an hourglass as a metaphor of unescaping situation. The narrative is about the relationship longing to go back to the beginning. The water represents those blurry and fluidity dynamics of time in memory space: sink and flow.
