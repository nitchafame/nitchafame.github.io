Dilation is an experimental visual sculpture inspired by a dream-like feeling and sensation of the human body into projection mapping on gigantic head paper sculpture. The videos that use to project on is using the face of a person and add a distorted layer using GLSL shaders on top. To make unreal effects that build a loose narrative to participations to fill.

 

The sculpture is part of Qualia exhibition at Livestream Bushwick  and Parsons Gala 2016
