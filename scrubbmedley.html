Visual Concert for SCRUBB

2010–Art Direction, Animation

 

Created parts of the Visual concert for the Thai pop indie band named “Scrubb.” Medley of two songs, the animations of a couple orbiting, exploring, and go around yet back to meet again. The artworks made using papercraft techniques, photographs, then animated and composited digitally.
