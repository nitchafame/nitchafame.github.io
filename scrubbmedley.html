Visual Concert for SCRUBB

2010 | Role : Art Director, Animator

 

Created parts of the Visual concert for Thai pop indie band named “Scrubb.” Medley of two songs, the animations metaphors of people orbiting, traveling and go around but back to see each other again. The artworks made using paper craft, photograph and digitally animated.
