DESIGN

The project started with thinking of the relationship between visual and sounds. The aesthetic is inspired from replicate aesthetic of cymatics, the beautiful symmetry visualizing audio frequencies, explores in fragment shader GLSL. The project also has an interactive aspect. It'd sound responsive done in Openframeworks.

 

I also inspired the style of the pattern by Julio Le Parc's work which usually found his work using repetition, fragmentation, and echoes in his works.
