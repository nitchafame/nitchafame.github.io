KID–SCRUBB

2011–Animation

 

The music video is following the name and lyric in the concept of ‘Kids.’ The word ‘kids’ had a homophone word in Thai means ‘think,’ combine kids. The music video expressed the imagination when we were young and how we see the world. Through shots in an amusement park, the place that represents childhood that overlayed cel animation is added a layer of playfulness as children’s imagination.
