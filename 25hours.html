Colour in White: 25 Hours

2011–Graphic Design

 

"Colour in White," the second album of Thai indie rock artist 25 hours. The band goes through overhaul graphical branding. The concept of color and white is to reflect the band's fresh and colorful music. The graphic pattern used inspires from visual of kaleidoscope camera where you can see things in front of you in a new colorful dimension. Which also provokes a sense of playful and bright. The outer cover is in white while unfolding the colorful booklet represents the vivid shades of music.
