Between Spaces Exhibition

2016 – Art Direction, Graphic Design

 

Between Spaces is the theme of thesis show at the Master of Fine Arts in Department of Design and Technology at Parsons School of Design in 2016. We created the typography, and the visual indicated the idea of in-between. The typography created from an analog slit-scan to extruded and distorted in 3 dimension shape plays with the idea of between digital and analog, between dimensions. The design lived across mediums from a theatre screen to a newsprint. We are worked as lead designer and art director, creating Visual and Identity design, and content strategy for annually thesis show.
