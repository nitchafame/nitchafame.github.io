108-1009 Muangnarak

2012–Lettering Design

 

 

 

Credits

Lettering design - Nitcha Tothong

Project - Krzysztov, RayMac TV

Client - Tourism Authority of Thailand

Cast - Chintnudda Lamakanond, Phatthi Buntawanit

 

108-1009 muangnarak is a television program about traveling by RayMac TV,  sponsored by the Tourism Authority of Thailand. A series of twelve letterings, each representing cities in Thailand. The design concept took an impression on the character of each city like a landmark, popular product, or architecture and mixed it with the mood of that chapter’s journey.
